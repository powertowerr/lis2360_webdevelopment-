Future of driving. It may be worth it!

New emerging and revolutionary technologies are developing faster than ever before, and the reality of autonomous cars is quickly approaching around the corner. Our future transportation seems to inevitably involve self-driving vehicles. Self-driving vehicles are cars or trucks in which human drivers are not required to take control to safely operate the vehicle. Most people are probably skeptical when first thinking about the idea of their car driving itself but there may be many benefits when it comes to this idea of driverless cars.

Technologically advanced. See how these cars work.

Driverless cars combine sensors and computer software to control, navigate, and drive the vehicle. Self-driving technologies have started being developed by multiple technology companies over the years, such as Google and Uber. Most designs for self-driving systems create and maintain an internal map of their surroundings, using a wide array of sensors. These sensors include lasers, radar, sonar, and high-powered cameras.

Impacts. How will this effect society.

Safety is a big concern when first thinking about driverless cars. Thousands of people die in motor vehicle crashes every year in the United States. Driverless cars could, hypothetically, reduce that death toll with computer software proving to be less error-prone than humans. However, the threat of cybersecurity and the full control of the vehicle is still a chief concern.
